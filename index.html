Privacy Policy

Publication Date: July 20, 2025

1. What data we collect

When placing an order in the app, we collect the following personal information:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Delivery address

This data is necessary solely for order processing and fulfillment.

2. How we use the data

The collected data is used only for the following purposes:

  • Contacting you regarding your order
  • Organizing delivery of your items
  • Customer support

3. Data sharing

Your personal data:

  • is not shared with third parties
  • is not sold
  • may be shared only with delivery services, if required to fulfill your order

4. Where the data is stored

The data is stored on a secure server and is accessible only to authorized personnel who manage the orders.

5. Data retention

We retain your data only for the time necessary to fulfill the order and provide possible follow-up support (e.g., returns, clarifications).

6. Security

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. The Service Provider provides physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect information the Service Provider processes and maintains.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your consent to data processing

To exercise your rights, please contact us at: support@example.com

8. Consent

By submitting the order form in the app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
